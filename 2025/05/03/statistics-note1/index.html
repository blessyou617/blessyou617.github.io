Statistical Reporting in User Research

Basic Statistical Terms

Mean and Standard Deviation

  • M (Mean): The arithmetic average of a group of values.
  • SD (Standard Deviation): A measure of how spread out the numbers are from the mean. A low SD indicates values are close to the mean, while a high SD indicates more variation.

Likert Scale

  • A psychometric scale commonly used in questionnaires.
  • Usually ranges from 1 (Strongly Disagree) to 7 (Strongly Agree).
